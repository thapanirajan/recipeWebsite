Procedures:
  • 1Prepare the Samosa Dough: Mix flour, salt, carom seeds, ghee/oil, and water. Knead to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  • 2Make the Samosa Filling: Saut√© cumin seeds, coriander seeds, onions, ginger, and green chilies. Add spices, potatoes, peas, and salt. Cook until well combined. Let it cool.
  • 3Shape and Fill the Samosas: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin oval shape. Cut it in half. Fold each half into a cone shape and seal the edges. Fill with the potato mixture. Seal the top
  • 4Deep-Fry the Samosas: Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the samosas on medium heat until golden brown and crispy. Drain on a paper towel.
  • 5Serve: Serve hot with chutneys or yogurt as accompaniments.
